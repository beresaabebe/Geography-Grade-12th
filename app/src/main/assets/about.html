Grade 12 th Geography Textbook

This app is all about Geography of Grade 12th Textbook This application is designed to help a number of people who want to study and improve their geography skills using these pocket book android application of Grade 12th Textbook

The application was designed and arranged according to the chapter from the first chapter which is chapter 01 to chapter 05 in the textbook. All the chapters are listed as follows

  • Basic research methodologies in geography
  • Map use and map work
  • Physical geography of ethiopia and the horn
  • Population of ethiopia and the horn
  • Economic growth and development trend in ethiopia
